Bookshelf

University project developed in 36 hours in collaboration with BUT MMI students.

It's a site on which you can create "you are the hero" type stories with possible choices that lead to different endings. You can completely create your story by adding chapters with text and a photo, video or audio and linking them together to make several possible paths. We can then find all the stories on the home page and read them as we wish by choosing our choices. There is also a login, register, logout functionality.

The BUT MMI students were in charge of the front part while we, the BUT Informatique students were in charge of the back.
